About This Tool

Welcome to Percentage Calculator, a simple, fast, and privacy-focused utility designed to help you solve everyday math problems instantly.

Our Mission

We believe that useful tools should be accessible, free, and easy to use. Many online calculators are cluttered with ads, require logins, or are slow to load. We built this tool to be the opposite:

  • Instant: Results appear as you type. No "Calculate" button needed.
  • Private: All calculations happen in your browser. No data is sent to any server.
  • Free: No subscriptions, no paywalls, ever.
