Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to Percentage Calculator ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from our users. Our percentage calculation tools run entirely in your browser (client-side), meaning the numbers you input never leave your device.

3. Local Storage

To improve your experience, we use your browser's "Local Storage" to remember the last values you entered. This allows you to close the tab and return later without losing your work. This data resides solely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

4. Analytics

We may use anonymous analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our site. This helps us improve functionality and user experience. These tools may use cookies to collect non-personal information like browser type, device type, and page views.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

6. Contact Us
